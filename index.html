WHO WE ARE

Serving Communities Since 1985

PHDFWF is a registered non-profit organisation promoted by PHDCCI, established under the Indian Trust Act, 1882, 80G of the IT Act and FCRA.

We operate 12 Mobile Medical Vans across Delhi, NCR, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Haryana and Gujarat, benefiting thousands of people every day.

Through our 34 skill development centres, we provide vocational training, education, women empowerment initiatives and community welfare programmes.
